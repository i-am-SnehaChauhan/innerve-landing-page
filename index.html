INNERVE

Innerve is an annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW), catering to the interests of numerous students across all fields.

Experiencing a footfall of over 9000 students from all over the country every year, Innerve is a platform where technical acumen blends with creativity and innovation.
